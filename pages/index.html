UNLAD

UNLAD is a student-initiated project created to support local businesses by providing free services such as system development to optimize operations, establishing social media presence, and assisting with digital transformation. Our first initiative, UNLAD – KAF, focuses on enhancing the campus indoor cafeteria experience by developing a full-stack system that allows scholars to view the menu and monitor their account balances through a custom-built app. In order to address the long lines in the cafeteria, payments will be processed through scanning scholars’ ID barcodes to record transactions. The system will maintain a database containing the menu, user balances, and purchase history, which is only accessible to authorized personnel. An optional feature will enable online ordering with later pickup. In the future, the project may also include automating the library check-in system, integrating all features into a single app tailored for students of PSHS CALABARZON-RC.
